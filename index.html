DustedClays

Hands-free clay target scoring on your Apple Watch.

Mount, call, shoot. DustedClays feels the recoil through your watch and scores every clay automatically — no buttons between shots, no soggy scorecards, no lost tallies.

⌚ Apple Watch  +  📱 iPhone

How it works

Three steps from gun case to scorecard.

1

Pick your discipline

Open the watch app and choose Trap, Skeet, Sporting, or any discipline you've built yourself. Tap Start — that's the last time you touch the screen.

2

Just shoot

The motion sensors detect each shot from recoil and score it a hit. Missed one? A single glance-free tap fixes it — with separate buttons for each bird of a pair.

3

Review on iPhone

Rounds sync to your iPhone automatically — every station, every clay, every second barrel. Watch your averages climb over the season.

Built for the range

💥

Automatic shot detection

Recoil is unmistakable to a wrist-worn motion sensor. Every shot registers the instant you take it — even with the screen down and your eyes on the trap.

🔁

Double-barrel & true pairs

A fast follow-up shot counts as your second barrel on the same clay. In Sporting, pairs score one shot per bird — with a split Miss button for each.

🛠️

Your disciplines, your rules

Build custom disciplines on the iPhone — stations, target sequence, shots per clay — and they appear on the watch automatically.

🎚️

Field calibration

A live G-force readout lets you tune detection to your gun, load and mount in a couple of shots. Set it once, forget it.

⏱️

Runs the whole round

The watch keeps sensing between stations and while your wrist is down on the gun — a full round tracks start to finish without waking the screen.

🔒

Private by design

Your scores live on your devices. No account, no analytics, no cloud — nothing leaves your watch and phone.

Every clay accounted for

Privacy

DustedClays collects no data. Full stop.

All scores and settings are stored locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch and sync only between your own devices. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising and no third-party SDKs, and it never transmits anything to us or anyone else. Motion-sensor data is processed on the watch in real time to detect shots and is never recorded or stored.

This page is the app's privacy policy. If it ever changes, the change will be visible right here.
